 ​Bully Beds: A Family-Owned Business

Bully Beds was created after Bull, my 150 pound Cane Corso mastif began to experience apparent hip discomfort in 2015.
Our vet told me that a dog’s bed can have a significant impact on their ability to sleep and rest comfortably because without sufficient support, the dogs’ joints come into contact with the hard surface under the bed.
After hours searching online for a bed that would fit Bull’s needs, I still had no solution. So I decided to make my own.
The mission was simple: I set out to develop an affordable, high quality dog bed that provides the comfort and support a big dog requires, enabling both luxurious relaxation and therapeutic relief.  The bed had to be waterproof, expertly made, attractive, and long lasting.  In fact, I wanted the bed to last for the dog’s entire life.
After several years of dedication and growth, I truly believe that we have created the world’s best dog bed, period.
To d ay, Bully Beds is trusted and used by over 44,000 dog owners worldwide. Yet we remain a small, family owned and operated business, based in sunny Florida.  If you’re ready to join us on our mission of improving our dogs’ overall quality of life, I recommend you give them the bed of their dreams - a Bully Bed.

- Joe Chanda, Founder & CEO
